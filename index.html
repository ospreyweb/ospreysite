Services

Sea Freight

As one of the well known Sea Freight Forwarders, Osprey offers sea freight solutions customized to your needs. We have a global network of highly skilled people who have a long-term relationship with all major shipping lines

Cross Trade

In an increasingly integrated global economy, more and more of our clients are arranging cross trade shipments; sourcing products and manufacturing opportunities outside their country of origin to then export to the rest of the world. At Osprey we understand that sometimes our customers need to move cargo to and from countries in which they themselves aren’t based. That’s why we’ve established our cross trades team, who can help with queries and bookings outside of the country from where your offices are situated. Our cross trades shipment services span all corners of the globe

Customs Clearance Service

Through our customs clearance service, we ensure timely delivery of goods to our customers. We undertake to prepare all the necessary documentation and pay relevant taxes and duties promptly to the authorities at the Indian ports. This will ensure that the goods are released quickly from these ports and hence, speedy delivery to the customers is guaranteed.
